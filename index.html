Start self-hosting in minutes

Use these steps as a template for your stack, then tailor them to your environment.

  1. 1

    Pick your base

    Deploy a small VM or container host. A single VPS with Docker or Podman is enough to prototype.

  2. 2

    Wire storage

    Start with SQLite or Postgres. Keep backups local and encrypted; layer in object storage later.

  3. 3

    Secure the edge

    Use a reverse proxy (Caddy, Nginx) with TLS, rate limits, and OAuth/OIDC if you plan shared access.

  4. 4

    Map your flows

    Sketch intake forms, data imports, and dashboards. This starter site is ready for your components.
