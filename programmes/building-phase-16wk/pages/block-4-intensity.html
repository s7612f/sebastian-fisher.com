Block 4 — Intensity & Taper · Weeks 13–16

Volume drops back to moderate. Every set counts — intensity techniques on most exercises. RPE 9–10. Week 16 is a taper.

Block 4 Philosophy

You've spent 12 weeks building volume tolerance. Now you cash it in. Lower total sets but maximum effort per set. Every working set should be within 1 rep of failure. Intensity techniques are applied systematically.
