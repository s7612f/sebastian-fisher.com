Block 1: Weeks 1–3 — Foundation

Establish your caloric deficit and build training momentum. Moderate volume, controlled effort. The goal is to dial in your nutrition, lock down your sleep, and get every movement pattern sharp before intensity ramps up. Pyramid every exercise: start light, finish heavy.

Target RPE: 7–8 on working sets (sets of 8 and 6).
Deficit: 400–500 kcal below maintenance.
Cardio: 2 × HIIT sessions, 15 minutes.
