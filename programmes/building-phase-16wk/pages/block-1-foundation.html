Block 1 — Foundation · Weeks 1–3

Arnold pyramid rep scheme: increase weight each set, decrease reps. Establish working weights. Focus on tempo and mind-muscle connection.

How to read the pyramid: "5 × 15,12,8,8,6" means 5 sets — start light at 15 reps, add weight each set, finish heavy at 6. Rest increases as weight goes up: 60 sec for 15s, 90 sec for 12s, 2 min for 8s and 6s.
