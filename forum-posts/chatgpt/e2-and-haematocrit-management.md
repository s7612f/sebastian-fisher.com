Tag: TRT / HRT (MEN)
Title: Managing oestradiol and haematocrit without knee‑jerk AIs
Author: CoachSebFisher
---

Principles:
- Dose/frequency first for E2 symptoms; avoid routine AI.
- Hydration, timing of blood draw, and lab method matter.
- HCT rising? Confirm hydration; consider phlebotomy only if above clinical thresholds and clinician agrees.
- Sleep apnoea and high body mass can push both E2 and HCT the wrong way — fix foundations.
