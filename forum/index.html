RECOMP LOG 2026 — SEB FISHER 11 Mar 2026, 08:14

Posting this here so there’s a record. Recomp block is done — 6 weeks Feb 3 to Mar 11. Full data below.

PRE: 2.8 sessions/week. 11.2 sets/session. 63-day gap in training at one point. 91.0kg.
RECOMP: 5.25 sessions/week. 19.7 sets/session. Zero missed sessions in 36 days. 89.0kg.

Arms +0.5 inch. OHP 40kg → 60kg. Hammer Curl DB 32.5 → 35kg. 540 exercise records in 36 days.

Cut starts now. 12 weeks. Easy deficit. Hold the size. Will log here.
