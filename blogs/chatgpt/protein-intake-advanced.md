Targets:
- 2.2–2.5 g/kg in a cut; 1.8–2.2 g/kg in maintenance/bulk.
- Distribute across 3–5 meals with 25–50 g high‑quality protein each.

Execution:
- Anchor meals around training. Add a slow‑digesting source pre‑sleep when cutting.
- Prioritise chewable protein; shakes are tools, not foundations.
- Travel: shelf‑stable kits (jerky, whey, tuna, rice cakes, nuts), hotel kettle oats + whey.

Digestion:
- If large meals bloat you, add an extra feeding. Enzyme products are last resort after trying simpler meals and chewing.
