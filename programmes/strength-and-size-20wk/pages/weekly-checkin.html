What to Do With This Data

  • Bodyweight not moving up after 2 weeks: add 100–200 kcal.
  • Gaining more than 0.6 kg/week: reduce by 100–150 kcal.
  • Lifts stalling for 2+ weeks despite eating: check sleep and stress first, then consider adding a rest day.
  • RPE creeping up on weights that felt easy before: you’re accumulating fatigue. Trust the deload.
