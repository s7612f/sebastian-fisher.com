JOIN THE NETWORK

Two ways to be part of Iron Gospel — as a qualified coach who receives referred clients, or as an affiliate who earns a fee for bringing clients in. Both are straightforward. No pyramid structures, no subscription fees.

▼ I AM A COACH

Qualified PT or specialist coach. Get referred clients based on your location, specialty, and current capacity. You keep your fee minus a small network commission.

► Referred clients matched to your specialty
► You set your own rates
► 10% commission on referred client fees
► Listed in the network directory
▼ I WANT TO REFER

Not a coach — but you know people who need one. Earn a flat referral fee for every person you send who becomes a paying client. No qualification required.

► £30 per converted paying client
► No minimum referrals required
► Personal network, social media, gym friends
► Paid monthly via bank transfer

HOW IT WORKS

FOR COACHES

  1. Submit your application above
  2. I review your qualifications and experience
  3. If approved, you're added to the network
  4. When a client's needs match your specialty and location, I refer them to you directly
  5. You take them on at your own rate
  6. 10% of the first month's fee comes back to the network as a referral commission

FOR AFFILIATES

  1. Submit your application above
  2. You receive a personal referral link
  3. Share it however works for you
  4. When someone uses your link and becomes a paying coaching client, you earn £30
  5. Tracked automatically, paid monthly
  6. No minimum referrals, no contract
WHAT COUNTS AS A CONVERSION: A referred person who signs up for 1-on-1 coaching or the 6-week overhaul programme, and completes at least one payment. Programme-only purchases (PDFs, meal plans) are not included in affiliate commissions.
