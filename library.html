Graphic Standards Manuals

Identity systems and brand guidelines — original or archival scans.

  • Bell System Graphic Standards Manual (1969) — Saul Bass; the Bell logo identity system
  • NASA Graphics Standards Manual (1975) — the worm era; original PDF scan
  • NASA Graphics Standards Manual (1976) — revised edition
  • NYC Transit Authority Graphics Standards Manual — Vignelli / Unimark International
  • NJ Transit Graphic Standards — New Jersey Transit identity programme
  • IBM Logo Standards — Paul Rand's 8-bar and 13-bar IBM identity
  • IBM Corporate Design Programme — 1990s–2000s IBM graphic standards
  • Apple Identity Guidelines (1987) — pre-Jobs return; rainbow logo era
  • Federal Design Improvement Program — US government identity system, Danne & Blackburn
  • Letterform Archive Online — 3,500+ digitised design artefacts including identity manuals

Things to Print

Databases and archives worth downloading and printing.

  • Google Patents — historical patent drawings; especially pre-1960 mechanical diagrams
  • NASA Image and Video Library — high-res mission photography, schematics, technical diagrams
  • SkyVector — sectional aeronautical charts; IFR en-route charts; beautiful cartography
  • USGS Topographic Maps — full-resolution US topo maps; printable PDFs
  • CIA Reading Room — declassified documents; Cold War cartography and technical intelligence
  • Biodiversity Heritage Library — natural history illustration; 19th-century botanical and zoological plates
  • Bitsavers — scanned manuals for vintage computers and electronics; schematics, engineering docs
  • TUHS — Unix Heritage Society — Unix source code, documentation, and system diagrams
  • Archive.org — Computer Magazines — scanned issues of Byte, Creative Computing, dr. dobb's, and others
