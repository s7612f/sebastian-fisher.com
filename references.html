Things to Print

  • Google Patents: historical patent drawings; especially pre-1960 mechanical diagrams
  • NASA Image and Video Library: high-res mission photography, schematics, technical diagrams
  • SkyVector: sectional aeronautical charts; IFR en-route charts
  • USGS Topographic Maps: full-resolution US topo maps, printable PDFs
  • CIA Reading Room: declassified documents; Cold War cartography and technical intelligence
  • Biodiversity Heritage Library: 19th-century botanical and zoological illustration
  • Bitsavers: scanned manuals for vintage computers and electronics; schematics, engineering docs
  • TUHS: Unix Heritage Society: Unix source code, documentation, and system diagrams
  • Archive.org: Computer Magazines: scanned issues of Byte, Creative Computing, dr. dobb's, and others
