Deload Week — Weeks 4, 8, 12

Swap to compound movements. Low volume, high intensity. 4 sessions in 6 days. RPE 8–9 on top sets.

Purpose: Recover from accumulated fatigue while maintaining (or testing) strength. You should feel fresh and strong by the end of deload week. If you still feel fatigued going into the next block, extend the deload by 2–3 days.
