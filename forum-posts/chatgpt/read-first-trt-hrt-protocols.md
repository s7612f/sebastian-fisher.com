What TRT is — medical replacement for symptomatic hypogonadism confirmed by bloods. Not a shortcut.

Start here:
- Baseline labs (two fasted AM draws): Total T, Free T (calc or ED), SHBG, LH/FSH, prolactin, E2 sensitive, HCT/Hb, FBC, CMP, lipids, TSH ± fT3/fT4.
- Typical start: 100–120 mg/wk Test E/C, split E3D–EOD. No routine AI.
- Re‑check at 6–8 weeks (steady state). Adjust by symptoms + labs.
- Watch‑outs: HCT >54% (address before titrating), unmanaged sleep apnoea, blood pressure, fertility planning.
- Post labs in Bloodwork & Labs for context. No sourcing/illegal supply discussion.

Not medical advice. Work with a competent clinician.
