What is it

A desktop PC at home running Linux. It does several things at once:

  • NAS — files, photos, and backups on USB drives
  • App server — ~25 self-hosted web apps running in Docker
  • VPN — all phone and laptop traffic routes through it, encrypted
  • DNS filter — Pi-hole blocks ads and trackers at the DNS level

VPN Stack — how it works

Three layers: WireGuard encrypts everything. ShadowSocks wraps it so ISPs can't identify it as VPN traffic via deep packet inspection — it looks like random noise. Pi-hole filters DNS so ads and trackers never even get a response.

On hostile networks (public WiFi, travel) — connect ShadowSocks first, then WireGuard on top. This way the VPN is undetectable even under active inspection.
