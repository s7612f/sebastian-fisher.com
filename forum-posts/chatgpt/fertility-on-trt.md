Tag: TRT / HRT (MEN)
Title: Fertility on TRT — hCG, enclomiphene, and planning
Author: CoachSebFisher
---

If future fertility matters:
- Discuss hCG or enclomiphene with your clinician from day one.
- Get a baseline semen analysis before starting.
- Avoid assuming recovery is instant if you later stop — plan timelines.
