Nutrition Guidelines

This programme demands fuel. You will not add meaningful size or strength in a caloric deficit. The goal is a modest, controlled surplus — enough to drive muscle growth without excessive fat gain.

GoalTarget
CaloriesMaintenance + 200–400 kcal/day
Protein1.8–2.2g per kg bodyweight (minimum 1.8g)
CarbohydratesFill remaining calories after protein and fat targets
Fat0.8–1g per kg bodyweight
Bodyweight trendAim for 0.25–0.5 kg gain per week

Meal Timing

  • Pre-training: carbohydrate-rich meal 1–2 hours before session.
  • Post-training: protein + carbs within 2 hours. 40–50g protein, 60–80g carbs.
  • Before bed: slow-digesting protein (cottage cheese, casein shake) if total protein is low.
  • Overall daily total matters more than precise timing. Hit your numbers.

Supplement Basics

  • Creatine monohydrate: 5g daily. No loading needed. Take it every day.
  • Protein powder: only if you cannot hit protein targets from food.
  • Vitamin D3 + K2: especially if limited sun exposure.
  • Everything else: unnecessary until the basics are locked in.
