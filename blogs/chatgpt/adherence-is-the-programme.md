---
Title: Adherence Is The Programme
Slug: adherence-is-the-programme
Date: 2026-03-14
Tags: [Mindset, Training]
Summary: The best plan is the one you can and will execute for months, not days.
Author: Sebastian Fisher
---

A brilliant split done for 9 days loses to a simple split done for 90. The number one predictor of results: weeks of consistent execution.

Build for adherence:
- Simple exercise selection you can set up in 60 seconds.
- Clear progressions. No guesswork at the rack.
- Guardrails: minimums you hit on bad weeks (2 upper, 2 lower; 120 g protein).

Schedule first. Optimise second.
