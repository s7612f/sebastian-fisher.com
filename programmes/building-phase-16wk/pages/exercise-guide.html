Section 4: Exercise Guide

Chest & Back Day — Key Cues

Incline DB Press

Set bench to 30°. Bring dumbbells down until upper arms are parallel to floor or slightly below. Don't bounce. Press up and slightly in — dumbbells should nearly touch at the top. Squeeze pecs at top for 1 second.

Cable Chest Fly

Set pulleys at shoulder height or slightly above. Step forward for stretch. Arms slightly bent — locked elbow angle throughout. Bring hands together at chest level, squeeze 2 seconds. Control the return.

Machine Chest Press

Adjust seat so handles align with mid-chest. Press without locking elbows. Slow 2-sec eccentric. If machine has converging arms, use it — better chest activation.

Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown

Grip 1.5× shoulder width. Lean back 15°. Initiate by depressing shoulder blades, then pull elbows down and back. Lower the bar to upper chest. Control the return — feel the stretch at the top.

Seated Cable Row

Sit upright, slight forward lean at start for stretch. Pull to lower chest/upper abdomen. Squeeze shoulder blades together for 1 second. Don't use momentum.

Straight-Arm Pulldown

Stand facing cable, rope or straight bar at high position. Arms locked straight. Pull down in an arc until hands reach thighs. Feel lats stretch overhead on the return. Do not bend elbows.

Shoulders & Arms Day — Key Cues

Seated DB Lateral Raise

Sit on bench edge, slight forward lean. Raise dumbbells out to sides, leading with elbows (not hands). Stop at shoulder height. Lower slowly — 3 seconds down. Don't use momentum.

Cable Lateral Raise

Stand side-on to low cable. Cable runs behind legs. Raise arm to shoulder height, hold 1 second. Lower slowly. This provides peak tension at the top where DBs lose it.

Incline DB Curl

Set bench to 45°. Let arms hang straight down — full stretch on biceps. Curl without moving upper arms. Squeeze at top. Lower slowly — this is the money part of the movement.

Cable Rope Pushdown

Elbows pinned to sides throughout. Push down and split the rope at the bottom. Squeeze triceps for 1 second. Control the return — don't let the cable yank your arms up.

Legs Day — Key Cues

Leg Press

Feet shoulder-width, mid-platform. Lower until knees are at 90° or slightly below. Don't round lower back off the pad. Press through whole foot. Don't lock out knees at top.

Hack Squat

Narrow stance, feet low on platform for quad bias. Go deep — arse to ankles if mobility allows. Heels elevated on a plate if needed. Control the eccentric.

Lying Leg Curl

Point toes (plantar flexion) to reduce calf involvement. Curl fully — try to touch pad to glutes. Hold peak contraction 1 second. Lower slowly, don't let it drop.
